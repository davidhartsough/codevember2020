Codevember 2020

Turn this into your own dope website.

Steps

  1. Download this template website as a .zip (but don't do this from your phone)
  2. Unpack/extract the project from the .zip
  3. Find the index.html file in the extracted folder, and double-click it to open it in your browser. The URL should look something like this in the tab's address bar: File: /Users/steve/Downloads/codevember/index.html. Lookin' good, right?!
  4. Download a text editor (AKA an IDE), assuming you don't already have one. Popular ones include: Sublime, VS Code, and Atom. They're all great; any of them will do. Pick your favorite.
  5. Open the entire folder (codevember2020) in your text editor
  6. Look at the index.html file's code, and replace "Codevember 2020" on line 60 with your name. Ex: <h1>Heat Wave Dave</h1>.
  7. Go back to your browser tab that has the index.html file open and refresh the page. You should see your name on the website now.
  8. Congratulations! You've just started coding! Keep changing things and seeing what happens.
  9. Think of something cool to build. Start googling how to do it in HTML, CSS, and JS. (Googling how to do stuff is 90% of what most web developers do on a regular basis. You're virtually a web dev now.)
  10. Finally, once you've built your dope website, you're gonna wanna put it on the internet and share it with me and the rest of the world (wide web).
